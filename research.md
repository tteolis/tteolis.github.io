# Research

### Stein Variational Dynamics & Sampling Flows
Singular-kernel SVGD; finite-particle convergence; long-time sampling; uniform-in-time propagation of chaos; Riesz and Bessel kernels.

### Boltzmann Perspective on Foundation Models
Stochastic in-context maps and interacting-particle formulations for approximating Boltzmann solution operators, with connections to foundation models for fluid and continuum dynamics.

### Kinetic & Hydrodynamic Limits of Collective Dynamics
Long-time behavior, kinetic and hydrodynamic descriptions (mean-field and hydrodynamic limits), and fluid–particle interaction systems (Euler Alignment; Fokker–Planck–Navier–Stokes).

### Neural Surrogates for Wave Propagation
Operator learning for wave scattering and fast three-dimensional Helmholtz simulation, with applications to subsurface imaging and full-waveform inversion.
